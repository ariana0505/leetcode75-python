## Reverse Bits (English)

Reverse the bits of a given **32-bit signed integer**.

### Example 1
**Input:** `n = 43261596`  
**Output:** `964176192`

**Explanation:**

| Integer | Binary |
|--------:|:-------|
| 43261596 | 00000010100101000001111010011100 |
| 964176192 | 00111001011110000010100101000000 |

### Example 2
**Input:** `n = 2147483644`  
**Output:** `1073741822`

**Explanation:**

| Integer | Binary |
|--------:|:-------|
| 2147483644 | 01111111111111111111111111111100 |
| 1073741822 | 00111111111111111111111111111110 |

### Constraints
- `0 <= n <= 2^31 - 2`
- `n` is even

**Entrada:** `n = 43261596`  
**Salida:** `964176192`

**Explicación:**

| Entero | Binario |
|-------:|:--------|

| Entero | Binario |
